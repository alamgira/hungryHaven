About

WHO WE ARE

We love everything to do with food! If it’s deep fried, creative, and a bit unusual, it’s got our attention. Whether it’s a food fusion, wacky new product, or an insane amount of food, we’re interested. With Hungry Haven, we want to bring our passion for crazy foods to the masses.

Hungry Haven is the ultimate go-to resource to find food challenges and contests near you. Whether you’re looking for something to do in your home city or curious while travelling, we’ll give you the best and the latest in amazing food feats in your area.

OUR MISSION

Our priority is to give users an easy and enjoyable way to discover food challenges. Instead of going through a ton of websites to find your next food challenge, we’ll do the work for you. We’ll save you time and keep you in the loop by showing you everything you need to know about food challenges and contests all on one page.

WHAT WE DO

Based on your location, we’ll show you food challenges and events that are close by. We’re constantly scouring the interwebs to give you the most up to date information. Whether you like burgers, sushi, or insanely spicy wings, there’s a challenge out there for you. Not into eating large amounts of food yourself? No problem. We’ll keep you updated on some of the best competitive eaters from around the globe to show you how it’s done.

With challenges and festivals in over 60 major cities across Canada and the US, our database has nearly 1000 challenges, and we’re still growing!
